üéØ QA Overview

Quality Assurance (QA) is a critical role in software development that ensures the quality, reliability, and performance of software products. QA professionals prevent defects, identify potential issues, and maintain high standards throughout the software development lifecycle. They collaborate with developers, product managers, and other stakeholders to ensure software meets user expectations and functional requirements.
